A fast, professional website for your business in Czechia

Running a business in Czech Republic as an expat or international company? You need a website that loads fast, looks professional, and helps Czech customers find you. A static website delivers all of this — without the overhead of complex CMS systems.

What you get

  • Google PageSpeed 95–100/100 — speed is a ranking factor in Google, and your visitors will notice
  • Zero security vulnerabilities — no database, no CMS, no attack surface for hackers
  • Bilingual by design — Czech and English versions built in, so you reach both local and international clients
  • SEO-ready from day one — proper meta tags, structured data, and fast loading for top Google rankings
  • Fully responsive — looks perfect on mobile, tablet, and desktop
  • No CMS maintenance costs — no plugin updates, no security patches, no downtime

Who it’s for

  • Expat entrepreneurs — consultants, coaches, freelancers who need a professional online presence
  • International companies — opening a Czech branch and need a local website
  • Startups — landing pages for product launches and investor presentations
  • Professional services — law firms, accounting, real estate, medical practices
  • Digital nomads — portfolio and service pages for your Czech-based business

How we work

  1. Consultation in English — tell us about your business, goals, and target audience
  2. Content strategy — we plan the site structure, pages, and keywords for the Czech market
  3. Design and development — we build a modern, responsive website on the Hugo platform
  4. SEO setup — we optimize for Google Czech Republic and set up Google Business Profile
  5. Launch and handover — we deploy to reliable hosting with SSL and walk you through everything

Why Hugo instead of WordPress?

Hugo is a static site generator used by companies worldwide. Unlike WordPress, it doesn’t need a database, PHP server, or constant updates. The result is a website that’s faster, more secure, and cheaper to maintain. If you don’t need daily content changes or an online store, a static site is the smarter choice.
